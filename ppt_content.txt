[Google Shape;177;p19]: "Software-Defined Continuity"

[Google Shape;179;p19]: 
[Google Shape;180;p19]: 
[Google Shape;181;p19]: 01
[Google Shape;182;p19]: H3 Hexagonal Indexing
[Google Shape;183;p19]: Spatial Mesh Architecture
[Google Shape;184;p19]: Treat Chennai as a digital mesh. By "fusing" live traffic data from the city's grid into the bus's specific hexagon, we adjust the ETA before the bus even hits the traffic jam.
[Google Shape;185;p19]: 
[Google Shape;186;p19]: 
[Google Shape;187;p19]: Uber H3 Library implementation
[Google Shape;188;p19]: 
[Google Shape;189;p19]: 
[Google Shape;190;p19]: 02
[Google Shape;191;p19]: Predictive Dead Reckoning
[Google Shape;192;p19]: GPS Dark Zone Navigation
[Google Shape;193;p19]: When a bus enters a "Signal Dark Zone" (GPS loss), our engine uses the last known speed and road geometry to predictively animate the bus icon. The user never sees a "stale" or "frozen" icon.
[Google Shape;194;p19]: 
[Google Shape;195;p19]: 
[Google Shape;196;p19]: Speed + geometry prediction
[Google Shape;197;p19]: 
[Google Shape;198;p19]: 
[Google Shape;199;p19]: 03
[Google Shape;200;p19]: Map-Matching (HMM)
[Google Shape;201;p19]: Hidden Markov Models
[Google Shape;202;p19]: Use Hidden Markov Models to "snap" messy GPS signals to the exact bus route, ensuring the location is always visually accurate on the road, not drifting into buildings or side streets.
[Google Shape;203;p19]: 
[Google Shape;204;p19]: 
[Google Shape;205;p19]: Probabilistic route snapping
[Google Shape;206;p19]: 
[Google Shape;207;p19]: 
[Google Shape;208;p19]: 04
[Google Shape;209;p19]: AI State Detection
[Google Shape;210;p19]: Ghost Bus Prevention
[Google Shape;211;p19]: To stop "Ghost Buses," our engine uses AI to detect if a bus is Stationary vs. Active. If a bus hasn't moved for 5 minutes at a terminal, it is automatically hidden from the "Nearby" view.
